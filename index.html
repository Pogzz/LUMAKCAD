BUILDING CHAMPIONS BEYOND COMPETITION

UMAK CENTER FOR ATHLETIC DEVELOPMENT

The Center for Athletic Development (CAD) is responsible for the promotion, development, and implementation of the sports program of the University of Makati by providing administrative directions in all its intercollegiate athletic activities which include recruitment, training, and holistic formation of student-athletes.

BE A CHAMPION

To realize the vision, ADC will develop a structured and organized program that will make the University of Makati the leading local educational institution in sports while enabling its student-athletes to reach their full potential and prepare them for lifelong success within an athletic organization that promotes well-rounded education through planned sports and competitions developing their skills, discipline, integrity, leadership, and social responsibility thus, making the athletic program a lasting source of pride for the University and the the city of Makati.

WHY PICK UMAK?

Our mission is to realize this vision by developing a structured and organized program that makes us the leading local educational institution in sports. We aim to help our student-athletes reach their full potential and prepare them for lifelong success. Because at the University of Makati, we don't just build athletes; We build champions.
